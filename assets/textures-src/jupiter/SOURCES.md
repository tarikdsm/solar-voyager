## Project-authored ring and detail layers

- `jupiter_rings.png` — radial structure and optical depths from https://pds-rings.seti.org/jupiter/jupiter_rings_table.html — NASA/PDS public data; project visualization — generated deterministically at 2048×64 by `tools/textures/generateRingTextures.mjs`.
- `jupiter_detail_albedo.jpg` — project-authored deterministic gas-band microvariation, seed from `data/bodies.json` — project-authored — generated at 1024×1024 by `tools/textures/generateGasDetailTextures.mjs`.
- `jupiter_detail_normal.png` — tangent-space derivative of the same deterministic gas field — project-authored — generated at 1024×1024.
- `jupiter.glb` — generated by `tools/blender/build_jupiter.py` from the catalogued Jupiter body and ring contract — project-authored geometry.
